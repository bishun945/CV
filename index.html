Main

Shun Bi 毕顺

My research interests include optical water classification, bio-geo-optical modeling, ocean color parameter retrieval, and atmospheric correction. Specifically, I am working on developing a blending algorithm capable of estimating optical active constitutes, such as Chlorophyll-a concentration, in both Case-1 and Case-2 waters using remote sensing data. I am also interested in studying column-integrated algal biomass in shallow lakes and data gap-filling for satellite images. Although I initially focused on water color remote sensing in inland waters, my current research encompasses all types of natural waters.
